## Algen Network

Algen leverages three core technology engines—ASDO, Mesher, and Veilchain—to achieve breakthroughs in the **depth, breadth, and precision** of blockchain network services. ASDO uses an AI engine to dynamically adjust the information processing and operational modes of the entire Algen L1+L2 network, ensuring Algen is always in optimal condition. Mesher provides a shared security and interoperability foundation for various customized L2 networks. Veilchain, through protocol-layer privacy mechanisms, provides end-to-end transaction encryption and compliant privacy protection for users and institutions within the ecosystem. These three core technologies work together to provide DApps with a high-performance, interoperable, and privacy-controlled full-stack development environment.

Through the synergy of its three core technologies, Algen Network provides developers with a high-performance, low-cost, privacy-controlled, and AI-enabled blockchain development platform. Whether you are a blockchain novice or a seasoned developer, this document will help you quickly get started and fully leverage Algen's powerful features.

Algen's core technological moat consists of three engines:

<Card icon="route" title="ASDO (Algen Self-Determined Option)">
  ASDO is the "brain" of the Algen network. It uses advanced AI models to perceive network load, transaction traffic, and market sentiment in real time, dynamically adjusting the allocation of L1 and L2 resources and consensus parameters. This not only ensures the stability of the network under extremely high throughput but also enables "autonomous evolution," allowing the blockchain to adapt to constantly changing environments like a living organism, always maintaining optimal operating conditions.
</Card>

<Card icon="chart-network" title="Mesher (Modular Interoperability Engine)">
  Mesher is Algen's "neural network." It constructs a decentralized L2 grid system, enabling developers to deploy highly customized application chains (AppChains) with a single click. Through a shared sequencer and a unified cross-chain messaging protocol, Mesher breaks down the "data silos" between traditional Rollups, achieving sub-second cross-chain interoperability and seamless asset transfer, providing limitless combination possibilities for complex application scenarios such as RWA (Real-World Assets), DeFi, and SocialFi.
</Card>

<Card icon="masks-theater" title="Veilchain (Protocol-Level Privacy Engine)">
  Veilchain is Algen's "shield." Through protocol-level privacy mechanisms, it constructs an end-to-end encrypted transaction pipeline—from Encrypted RPC and Encrypted Mempool to Ciphertext Storage—ensuring that plaintext data never appears in public memory throughout the entire transaction lifecycle. Veilchain implements quadruple privacy protection for sender, receiver, amount, and data, while also incorporating an encrypted mempool to eliminate malicious MEV attacks and providing tiered information disclosure capabilities to meet compliance and regulatory requirements, providing a privacy foundation for the secure on-chain storage of institutional assets.
</Card>
